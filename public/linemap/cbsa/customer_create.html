9 * This program takes customer information from the BMS
10 * application (name, address and DOB) and should get the SORTCODE,
11 * determine which datastore to use (VSAM or DB2), then enqueue
12 * the counter for CUSTOMER, increment the counter and get
13 * that number.
14 *
15 * It then performs a credit check on multiple
16 * credit agencies (using Async API), waits for 3 seconds and
17 * aggregates and averages the returned credit scores.
18 *
19 * If no data is returned, from the credit checks, then
20 * set the credit score to 0 and mark the credit
21 * score review date as today (so another attempt at credit
22 * scoring will be carried out later).
23 *
24 * Next, attempt to update the CUSTOMER datastore & if that is
25 * successful, write a rec to the PROCTRAN datastore.
26 *
27 * If all of that works, then DEQUEUE the named counter
28 * and return the SORTCODE and CUSTOMER number.
29 *
30 * If for any reason the write to the CUSTOMER or PROCTRAN
31 * datatsore is unsuccessful, then we need to decrement the Named
32 * Counter (restoring it to the start position) and DEQUEUE the
33 * Named Counter.
34 *
35 ******************************************************************
